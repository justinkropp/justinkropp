Justin Kropp is a Designer. He is currently a staff product designer at Vercel. Previously, he held staff product design and leadership roles at Modern Treasury, Okta, Mozilla, Automattic, and Readmill.

He maintains a small design practice, producing self-initiated work that is primarily auto-ethnographic in approach, connecting personal experiences with the wider world through an art and design lens. Themes that wander through this work include: design systems, typography, the written word, form, nature, music, and emotion.
