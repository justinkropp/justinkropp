Hi, my name is Justin Kropp — a software designer. I've had the chance to work in design and leadership roles at some great companies, including Vercel, Modern Treasury, Okta, Mozilla, Automattic, and Readmill.

Alongside that, I run a small design practice where I explore self-initiated work. A lot of it is personal in nature — drawing from my own experiences and connecting them to the wider world through art and design. The things that keep pulling me back: design systems, typography, the written word, form, nature, music, and emotion.
